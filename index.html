Quality & Testing

BLUSPAAR’s long-term quality model is built around lot identification, testing, validation, and disciplined handling for premium fruit.

Planned quality framework
  • Pesticide residue testing
  • Microbial testing
  • Heavy metal screening where needed
  • Brix, firmness, and visual grading
  • Batch-based traceability records
Why this matters

Premium consumers increasingly expect more than attractive packaging. They want transparency, safety, and trust behind what they eat.

QR Traceability

Our vision is for each pack to connect to its own digital journey. A simple scan can help consumers understand where the berries came from and how they were handled.

What a QR page can show
  • Source or farm location
  • Harvest date and lot code
  • Testing summary
  • Cold-chain handling status
  • Brand story and quality promise

Cold Chain

Blueberries depend on careful temperature control. A premium berry experience is only possible with fast pre-cooling, disciplined storage, and proper transit handling.

Rapid pre-cooling

Fast post-harvest cooling helps preserve texture, bloom, and shelf life.

Controlled storage

Low, stable temperatures are critical for maintaining freshness and visual quality.

Premium delivery

Cold-chain discipline supports both shelf appeal and long-term brand trust.
